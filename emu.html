Frequently Asked Questions

Is emulation legally protected?

Yes. Emulating hardware is entirely legal. However, piracy—downloading copyrighted ROMs or ISOs you do not physically own—is strictly prohibited and against Aetherlink's policies. Always dump your own software.

How can I resolve performance stuttering?

Ensure your emulator's graphics API is set to Vulkan (where applicable), compile your shader caches correctly, and verify your operating system is operating in a High Performance power state.

Do I need a powerful PC to emulate these consoles?

Older systems like DS and N64 run on almost anything, but Switch and Wii U require modern multi-core CPUs and dedicated GPUs.

Can I play multiplayer online?

Yes, many emulators support online play via custom servers or local network simulation like Netplay.
